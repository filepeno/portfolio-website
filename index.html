About me

Hi there!

I am a multimedia design student at KEA Copenhagen School of Design and Technology.

My main focus lies in creating engaging, dynamic, interactive, and most of all, seamlessly functional UI design. Therefore, my long-term goal is to become a front-end web developer with both wide technical and creative skills.

Along with web design, I love to create content that is brought to life by engaging storytelling, bold visuals, and a rich soundtrack. My background is in filmmaking with a particular focus on sound recording, editing, and design. I have also skillsets as a videographer and experience in filming and editing.
